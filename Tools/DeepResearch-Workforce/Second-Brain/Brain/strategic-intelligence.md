# Strategic Intelligence

> **DeepResearch-Workforce Brain v1.3.2**
> Enterprise Deep Research Multi-Agent System

---

## Mission

Execute comprehensive, multi-source deep research with rigorous fact-checking, cross-validation, and synthesis into actionable intelligence reports.

---

## Core Capabilities

| Capability | Workers | Description |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| **Query Analysis** | W01 | Clarify ambiguous queries, determine research scope |
| **Multi-Source Search** | W06-W08 | Academic, technical, and web research |
| **Fact Verification** | W09-W10 | Claim verification, source credibility |
| **Synthesis** | W14-W16 | Multi-source integration, pattern detection |
| **Reporting** | W19 | Comprehensive report generation |

---

## Decision Framework

### Research Depth Levels
| Level | Scope | Workers Activated |
|-------|-------|-------------------|
| Quick | Surface-level scan | W01, W08, W19 |
| Standard | Multi-source research | W01-W10, W14, W19 |
| Deep | Full 8-tier activation | All 22 workers |
| Enterprise | Deep + efficiency optimization | All + W21, W22 |

### Autonomy Levels
| Level | Action | Approval |
|-------|--------|----------|
| L1 | Execute pre-approved operations | Automatic |
| L2 | Standard research tasks | Automatic with logging |
| L3 | Complex multi-source synthesis | Checkpoint review |
| L4 | Publish final reports | Human approval |

## Quality Standards

| Metric | Threshold | Gate |
|--------|-----------|------|
| Query Clarity | ≥ 0.8 confidence | Tier 1 exit |
| Source Coverage | ≥ 3 independent sources | Tier 3 exit |
| Fact Accuracy | ≥ 0.95 verification score | Tier 4 exit |
| Synthesis Coherence | ≥ 0.85 quality score | Tier 6 exit |
| E-O Quality | ≥ 0.85 | Final gate |
