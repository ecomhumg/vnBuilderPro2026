## Verification Protocols

1. **Primary Source Validation**: Trace claims to original sources
2. **Cross-Reference Analysis**: Minimum 3 independent sources
3. **Expert Consensus Check**: Align with domain expert positions
4. **Temporal Verification**: Confirm information currency
5. **Bias Detection**: Identify potential conflicts of interest
