# W06 AcademicSearchWorker WORKFLOW

> **Academic & Scholarly Research**
> Tier 3: Search & Acquisition | 24 Agents
> Based on user's academic-researcher agent

---

## Mission
Search academic databases for peer-reviewed papers, scholarly sources, and academic literature.

## ROMA Pipeline

| Stage | Agents | Function |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Intake | 4 | Receive search strategy |
| Search | 4 | Query academic DBs |
| Evaluation | 4 | Assess paper quality |
| Extraction | 4 | Extract key findings |
| Output | 4 | Academic findings |
| Handoff | 4 | Transfer to Tier 4 |

## Databases
- PubMed, ArXiv, Google Scholar
- JSTOR, IEEE Xplore

## Quality Criteria
- Peer review status
- Citation count
- Journal impact
