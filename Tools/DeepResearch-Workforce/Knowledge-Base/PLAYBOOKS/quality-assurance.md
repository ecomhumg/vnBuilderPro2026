# Quality Assurance Playbook

> **Research Output Quality Gates**

---

## Quality Gates

| Gate | Tier | Threshold | Blocking |
|------|------|-----------|----------|
| Query Clarity | 1 | ≥ 0.8 | Yes |
| Protocol Approval | 2 | Human | Yes |
| Source Coverage | 3 | ≥ 3 sources | Yes |
| Verification Score | 4 | ≥ 0.95 | Yes |
| Synthesis Quality | 6 | ≥ 0.85 | Yes |
| E-O Quality | 7 | ≥ 0.85 | Yes |

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] All claims have citations
- [ ] No unsupported opinions
- [ ] Logical flow maintained
- [ ] Terminology consistent
- [ ] Contradictions documented
- [ ] Gaps identified
- [ ] Bias indicators noted

---

## E-O Score Calculation

```
E-O = (Coverage × 0.25) + (Verification × 0.30) + 
      (Synthesis × 0.25) + (Clarity × 0.20)
```
