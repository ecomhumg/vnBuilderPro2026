# Validation Heuristics Experience

> **Verification Wisdom from Fact-Checking**

---

## Quick Validation Checks
1. Does the source exist?
2. Is the quote accurate?
3. Is context preserved?
4. Are dates current?
5. Is author credible?

## Red Flag Patterns
- "Studies show" without citation
- Anonymous expert quotes
- Extraordinary claims without evidence
- Emotional manipulation language

## Trust Calibration
- High trust: Peer-reviewed, multi-sourced
- Medium trust: Single reputable source
- Low trust: Unverified, commercial interest
