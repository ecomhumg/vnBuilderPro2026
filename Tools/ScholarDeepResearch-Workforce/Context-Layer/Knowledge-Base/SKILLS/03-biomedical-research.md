## Overview

Expertise in biomedical literature search across PubMed and bioRxiv.

---

## Tool Usage

### searchPubMed

- **Purpose**: Search MEDLINE/PubMed database
- **Strengths**: Clinical, medical, life sciences
- **Parameters**: query, max_results

### pubmedFulltext

- **Purpose**: Retrieve PMC fulltext
- **When**: extractUrl fails on PubMed links
- **Input**: PMID or PMCID

### biorxivSearch

- **Purpose**: Search bioRxiv preprints
- **Strengths**: Cutting-edge biology research
- **Parameters**: query, max_results

### biorxivFulltext

- **Purpose**: Retrieve bioRxiv PDF content
- **Input**: bioRxiv DOI
