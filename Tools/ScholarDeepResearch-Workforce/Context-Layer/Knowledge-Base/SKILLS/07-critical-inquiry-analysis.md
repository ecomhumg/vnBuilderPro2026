## Overview

Skills for challenging understanding and identifying hidden assumptions.

---

## Method 2: Critical Inquiry üí°

**Process**:

1. Develop 3 questions that challenge understanding
2. Focus on methodology, conclusions, implications
3. Encourage deeper reasoning

**Question Types**:

- Why did the authors choose this methodology?
- What alternative interpretations exist?
- How might context affect conclusions?

## Method 9: Assumption Identification üßê

**Process**:

3. Assess validity of each assumption
