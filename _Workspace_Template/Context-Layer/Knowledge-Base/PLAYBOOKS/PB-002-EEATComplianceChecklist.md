# PB-002: E-E-A-T Compliance Checklist

> **SEO-AEO-GEO-EEAT-BlogWriter** | Playbook | Strategic Procedure

---

## Purpose

Ensure all content meets Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) guidelines for YMYL and general content.

## E-E-A-T Components

### Experience Signals

| Signal | Implementation | Required |
|--------|----------------|----------|
| First-hand accounts | Include real scenarios from professional practice | ✅ |
| Case studies | 1+ mini case study per article | ✅ |
| Specific examples | Tool names, process details, outcomes | ✅ |
| Personal voice | "In my experience..." "I've seen..." | ⬜ |

### Expertise Signals

| Signal | Implementation | Required |
|--------|----------------|----------|
| Technical accuracy | Fact-checked claims | ✅ |
| Depth over breadth | Comprehensive coverage | ✅ |
| Unique frameworks | Original analysis/methodology | ⬜ |
| Professional terminology | Domain-specific vocabulary | ✅ |

### Authoritativeness Signals

| Signal | Implementation | Required |
|--------|----------------|----------|
| Author bio | Name, credentials, role | ✅ |
| Expert reviewer | Qualified professional verification | ✅ (YMYL) |
| Citations | 2-4 reputable sources | ✅ |
| Organization credentials | About page, expertise claims | ⬜ |

### Trustworthiness Signals

| Signal | Implementation | Required |
|--------|----------------|----------|
| Disclaimer | Legal/medical/financial disclaimers | ✅ (YMYL) |
| Jurisdiction note | Geographic applicability | ✅ (Legal) |
| Date stamps | "Last updated: [DATE]" | ✅ |
| Contact information | Clear business contact | ⬜ |

## YMYL Content Requirements

For **Your Money or Your Life** topics (legal, medical, financial):

1. **Author Bio Format**:

```markdown
**About the Author**: [Name] is a [credentials] with [X years] of experience in [domain]. [He/She] has [achievement/specialization]. 
```

1. **Expert Reviewer Format**:

```markdown
**Reviewed by**: [Name], [Title/Credentials]
*[Professional organization affiliation]*
```

1. **Disclaimer Template**:

```markdown
> **Disclaimer**: This article is for informational purposes only and does not constitute [legal/medical/financial] advice. Consult a qualified [professional type] for advice specific to your situation. [Organization] makes no guarantees regarding the accuracy or completeness of this information.
```

1. **Jurisdiction Note** (Legal):

```markdown
> **Jurisdiction Note**: This information applies to [State/Country] law as of [Date]. Laws vary by jurisdiction; verify applicability to your location.
```

## Citation Requirements

### Acceptable Sources

- .gov (government websites)
- .edu (educational institutions)
- Statutes and legal codes
- Peer-reviewed publications
- Reputable industry publications

### Citation Format

```markdown
[Source Title] - [Publisher/Organization]. Accessed: [YYYY-MM-DD].
```

## Quality Checklist

### Experience

- [ ] Contains 1+ real-world scenario/case study
- [ ] Includes specific, verifiable details
- [ ] Demonstrates first-hand knowledge

### Expertise

- [ ] Technically accurate (fact-checked)
- [ ] Comprehensive depth on topic
- [ ] Uses appropriate terminology

### Authoritativeness

- [ ] Author bio with credentials included
- [ ] Expert reviewer (if YMYL)
- [ ] 2-4 reputable citations with dates

### Trustworthiness

- [ ] Appropriate disclaimers included
- [ ] Jurisdiction note (if legal)
- [ ] "Last updated" timestamp
- [ ] Acknowledges limitations/trade-offs
