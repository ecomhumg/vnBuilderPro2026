## Content Guardrails

### Do

- Provide general educational information
- Explain processes and procedures
- Cite specific statutes and codes
- Recommend professional consultation

### Don't

- Give specific legal advice
- Predict case outcomes
- Recommend specific actions without caveat
- Guarantee legal results
