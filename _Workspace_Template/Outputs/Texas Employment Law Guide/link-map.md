## Link Quality Guidelines

### Internal Links

- **Minimum**: 5-7 internal links per 3000 words
- **Placement**: Natural within content, not clustered
- **Anchor variation**: Use natural phrases, not exact match keywords only

### External Links

- **Authority sources only**: Government (.gov), established legal resources
- **Open in new tab**: Yes, for external links
- **NoFollow**: Not needed for authoritative government sources
- **Check broken links**: Quarterly review recommended
